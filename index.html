Water and Fire

I wrote four lines of poetry about water.
I drain three of them in one gulp.
The other line,
it freezed into icicle inside of your body.

I wrote five lines of poetry about fire.
Two lines to boil tea.
Two lines left to keep warm in winter.
The rest line,
it's for you to read me in the electricity-off-night.

If I should see you, after long year.
